Hello, everyone! I come from Kaohsiung. It is very convenient for me to go to ISU by riding the motorcycle. It just spends me about 30 minutes to go to ISU. My first day in ISU was very boring and tired because there was a very long speech on that day. Just kidding. I felt excited after I went around my campus. By the way, one of my roommates is my best friend because we have known each other since we studied in FHSH. Surprisingly, we study in the same college and meet each other again. 說實話這是我之前做英文報告所打的，不過配合介紹，還蠻符合的。
